About Me

I am a sophomore and a part of the class of 2028. My name is Jonah.

Learning is a big part of who I am, as I have mastered none of my current hobbies. I am learning guitar (along with some other instruments) and I play in a band called Carrey Powers. We create and cover rock music and have a lot of fun playing and learning together. It is also really great "team building" as we have to agree on everything we do.

"A band is like a marriage, and if you're in a marriage with someone, and you lose yourself in that marriage, the relationship is over, really," - Richie Sombora

I am extremely interested in studying computer science, and specifically AI. I find it to be extremely interesting and I believe that AI is the future. It is not something that should scare people and it certainly is not something that people should worry about. It makes us more efficient as a society, and if it takes jobs from people, those people will adapt. It will not be an easy or quick change for us, but it will be definite and sure.

I also play and am learning rugby. It is exciting to feel improvements after each practice and game with my team. I am not even close to the best on the team, however it is extremely fun to learn and to build connections with my teammates.
